Understanding 100¬∞C Oven Temperature

An oven temperature of 100¬∞C is classified as a very low heat setting. It is not intended for standard baking or roasting, but rather for gentle cooking processes that require minimal heat. Many home cooks use 100¬∞C for warming food without continuing to cook it, drying herbs, dehydrating fruits, or keeping meals warm before serving. Because the heat is so low, food will not brown or crisp at this temperature.
