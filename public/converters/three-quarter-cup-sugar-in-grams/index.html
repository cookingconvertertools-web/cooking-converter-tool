How to Measure 3/4 Cup Sugar Correctly

1

Confirm Sugar Type

Always check whether the recipe mentions granulated, brown, or powdered sugar, because 3/4 cup weight changes significantly.

2

Use a Standard Cup

Three quarter cup must be measured using a proper measuring cup, not a household mug or tea cup.

3

Level the Sugar

Sugar should sit loosely in the cup and be leveled gently, not pressed down.

4

Convert to Grams

Multiply 0.75 by 200 grams to reach the correct 150 gram value.

5

Check with Scale

Using a scale removes doubt and improves repeatability in baking.
